# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two equal audiences: (1) recruiters and HR teams evaluating candidates for full-stack / mobile dev roles; (2) clients and businesses seeking freelance or contract work. Both visit with intent to quickly assess capability and decide whether to reach out. Decision is made in under 60 seconds — first impression is everything.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Ramadhan (314dhan): proves full-stack capability through the site itself. Success = visitor reaches the contact section convinced of competence and reaches out.

## Brand Personality

Sharp · Minimal · Technical

Emotional goals: instill confidence and precision. The visitor should feel: "This person knows exactly what they're doing." No fluff, no noise. Competence is communicated through restraint, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Startup landing page aesthetics; this is editorial-technical (developer docs meets art direction)
- No glassmorphism, no gradient text, no glow-on-dark flourishes beyond the functional cursor ambient
- No motion-as-performance; reveals and state changes only

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint signals competence** — every element must justify its existence; decoration is suspect
2. **Hierarchy over decoration** — size, weight, and whitespace create emphasis before color does
3. **Motion earns trust** — animations are fast, purposeful, and respect reduced-motion preference
4. **Information density is a feature** — don't hide skills behind hover states; recruiters scan quickly
5. **Dual-audience clarity** — employer sees reliability and depth; client sees range and creativity

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` on every animation
- Dark mode primary, light mode clean — both feel intentional
- Typography-first hierarchy: Fraunces (display) + Plus Jakarta Sans (body)
